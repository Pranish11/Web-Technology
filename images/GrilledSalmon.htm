Grilled Salmon Recipe

By Aussie Meat

Simple is the best! The key to this recipe is not to overcook the salmon, allowing the natural flavours to shine through, and finishing it with a touch of tangy lemon juice for a refreshing and balanced dish.

Prep Time:
10 mins

Cook Time:
10 mins

Total Time:
20 mins

Yield:
4 servings

Ingredients

  • 4 salmon fillets (~150g each)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Instructions

  1. Preheat your grill to medium-high heat.
  2. Pat the salmon fillets dry with paper towels and brush them lightly with olive oil. Season both sides evenly with salt and pepper.
  3. Place the salmon fillets skin-side down on the preheated grill grates. Grill for 4-5 minutes per side, or until the salmon flakes easily with a fork and reaches your desired doneness.
  4. Transfer the grilled salmon fillets to a serving plate. Squeeze fresh lemon juice over the top just before serving.
  5. Serve the simple grilled salmon immediately, with the remaining lemon wedges on the side. Enjoy the bright, citrusy flavour that the lemon juice adds to the delicate salmon.
